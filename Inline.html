Pets

Hamsters are rodents belonging to the subfamily Cricetinae, which contains 19 species classified in seven genera. They have become established as popular small pets.

Pet turtles are quiet and relatively undemanding, which makes them an appealing option as a companion.

A parakeet is any one of many small- to medium-sized species of parrot, in multiple genera, that generally has long tail feathers.

Flowers

Dahlia is a genus of bushy, tuberous, herbaceous perennial plants native to Mexico and Central America.

Tulips are spring-blooming perennial herbaceous bulbiferous geophytes in the Tulipa genus.

Food

Pinapaitan or papaitan (lit. "to [make] bitter") is a Filipino-Ilocano stew made with goat meat and offal and flavored with its bile, chyme, or cud (also known as papait).

Adobo is the immersion of food in a stock (or sauce) composed variously of paprika, oregano, salt, garlic, and vinegar to preserve and enhance its flavor.

Tofu Sisig is a dish that's made of deep-fried tofu with a creamy and umami sauce. It is a little spicy and perfect with warm white rice.
