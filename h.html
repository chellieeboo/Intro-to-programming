High Protein Plan

Support muscle growth and recovery with a high-protein diet designed for active lifestyles.

Key Guidelines

  • Consume 1.2 to 2.0 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight daily.
  • Include lean meats like chicken breast and fish.
  • Incorporate plant-based proteins such as beans and tofu.
  • Don't forget healthy fats and carbohydrates to provide sustained energy.
