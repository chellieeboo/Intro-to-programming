Types Of Fruits

Grapes
A grape is a fruit, botanically a berry, of the deciduous woody vines of the flowering plant genus Vitis. Grapes are a non-climacteric type of fruits

  • Benefits:Boosts Your Immunity,Provides Helpful Antioxidants,Keeps You Hydrated
  • Scientific Name:Vitis

Banana
A long curved fruit which grows in clusters and has soft pulpy flesh and yellow skin when ripe. "a bunch of bananas"

  • Benefits:Bananas contain fiber, potassium, folate, and antioxidants, such as vitamin C.
  • Scientific Name:Musa

    • Apple
    An apple is a round, edible fruit produced by an apple tree Apple trees are cultivated worldwide and are the most widely grown species in the genus Malus.

  • Benefits:A good source of nutrients, including fiber, vitamin C, and antioxidants which can help support healthy digestion, brain health, and weight management
  • Scientific Name:Malus Domestica

    • Watermelon
    Watermelon is a flowering plant species of the Cucurbitaceae family and the name of its edible fruit. A scrambling and trailing vine-like plant, it is a highly cultivated fruit worldwide, with more than 1,000 varieties.

  • Benefits:Help move blood through your body and can lower your blood pressure
  • Scientific Name:Citrullus Lanatus

    • Strawberry
    The garden strawberry is a widely grown hybrid species of the genus Fragaria in the rose family, Rosaceae, collectively known as the strawberries, which are cultivated worldwide for their fruit.
