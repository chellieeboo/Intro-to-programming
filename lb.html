Low Carb Plan

This diet reduces carbohydrates to help promote fat loss while maintaining energy levels. It is ideal for people aiming to lose weight or manage blood sugar.

Diet Guidelines
  • Limit intake of bread, pasta, and sugary foods
  • Eat plenty of vegetables and leafy greens
  • Consume healthy fats such as avocado, nuts, and olive oil
  • Include moderate amounts of protein
